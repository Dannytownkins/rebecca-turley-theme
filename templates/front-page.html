Mercier Therapy

A leading approach in women's visceral manipulation.

Developed by Dr. Jennifer Mercier, this deep visceral manipulation technique is designed to restore mobility and blood flow to the reproductive organs.

Focus on Biomechanics, Not Just Chemistry

While conventional treatments often focus solely on hormones, this protocol targets the biomechanics of the pelvis. We operate on the principle that for any organ to function optimally—whether the uterus or ovaries—it must be able to move freely without restriction.

Release Tension & Restore Flow

This non-invasive therapy effectively releases soft tissue tension and visceral restrictions in the abdomen, hips, and spine. By addressing chronic tightness and scar tissue, we improve organ alignment and vascularity, ensuring reproductive organs aren't mechanically tethered.

A Structural Foundation for Fertility

Structured as a six-week program, this therapy targets the physical roots of fertility challenges like endometriosis, PCOS, or surgical recovery. When paired with IVF or IUI, it ensures the pelvic environment is mechanically sound—not just chemically stimulated.

Ultimately, Mercier Therapy bridges the gap between medical intervention and structural repair. By resolving the mechanical causes of pelvic dysfunction, you provide your body with the functional stability it needs to thrive.

This therapy is commonly used to assist with:

  • Infertility
  • C-Section recovery
  • Pelvic Pain
  • PCOS and Endometriosis
  • Hysterectomy Recovery

Why circulation matters

By focusing on restoring movement and circulation in the abdominal and pelvic regions, Mercier Therapy helps rebalance the body and promote natural healing. Healthy blood circulation is essential for optimal reproductive function.

Improved blood flow to the uterus, ovaries, and surrounding structures ensures that these organs receive adequate oxygen and nutrients—supporting menstrual health, fertility, and overall reproductive well-being.
